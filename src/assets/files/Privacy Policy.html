Some Cookies may last for a defined period of time, others last indefinitely. Your web browser should allow you to delete any you choose. It also should allow you to prevent or limit their use.

Our Website uses Cookies and our Cookies may store information including, but not limited to your use of the Website, registration to the Token Sale and account information.

When you visit our Website, we ask you whether you wish us to use Cookies; you are always free to decline our Cookies if your browser permits, but such disabling may hinder your ability to use some of the Website’s features. (a)          To track how you use our Website, and Services;
